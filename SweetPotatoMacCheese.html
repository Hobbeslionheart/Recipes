Sweet Potato Mac & Cheese

Author: Not Now

Publisher: Testing

Description: Mixing sweet potato into mac and cheese adds flavor, nutrition, and creaminess—and it’s also just really delicious. Use a store-bought sweet potato puree or roast your own. This works with butternut squash puree too!

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 8 servings

Category: Dinner

Cuisine: American

Keywords: mac and cheese, sweet potato, kid-friendly, vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 pound small pasta (such as mini gears, stars, elbows, or shells)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta and cook according to the package directions.

Notes

  • Add a pinch of crushed rosemary for additional flavor.

Serving Size: 1 serving
