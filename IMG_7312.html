Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Easy, tasty meatballs made with frozen meatballs and a sweet-and-sour sauce. Works well in a pressure cooker and reheats nicely. Serve over rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds (32 ounces) frozen meatballs
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions

Add all ingredients to pressure cooker, stir well and securely lock on lid. Set the cooker to high and cook for 5 minutes.

Let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes before (carefully!) quick releasing the remaining pressure and safely removing lid. Serve over rice.

Note / Tip

Bob's tip: To make your own porcupine meatballs in place of the frozen meatballs: combine 1 pound ground beef with 1/2 cup uncooked long grain white rice, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper. Increase cooking time by 1 minute.

Serves: 6

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 5 minutes
